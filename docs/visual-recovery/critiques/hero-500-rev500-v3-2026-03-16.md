# Critique: hero-500 (revision 500-v3)

**Date:** 2026-03-16T20:40:09.367Z
**Model:** llava:13b
**Source:** docs/visual-recovery/hero-sprite/hero-500.png

---

 1. FIRST GLANCE: This image reads as a knight.

2. BIGGEST PROBLEM: The single biggest visual problem is the pixelation and lack of detail, which makes the image look blocky and unrefined.

3. TOP 3 CHANGES:
   - Increase the resolution to provide more detail and reduce pixelation.
   - Enhance the shading and highlights to give the armor and cape more depth and texture.
   - Refine the facial features to convey more emotion and character.

4. SILHOUETTE TEST: If all interior color was removed and only the outline remained, the shape would still read as a knight. The silhouette is clear and the shape is distinctive enough to be recognized.

5. SCORES:
   - First-glance read: 3/5 - The image is recognizable as a knight, but the pixelation detracts from the readability.
   - Silhouette: 4/5 - The silhouette is clear, but the lack of detail makes it less effective.
   - Proportion: 3/5 - The proportions are generally correct, but the pixelation affects the smoothness of the lines.
   - Pose/stance: 3/5 - The pose is correct for a knight, but the pixelation limits the level of detail that can be seen.
   - Value separation: 3/5 - There is some value separation, but the pixelation makes it less distinct.
   - Material cues: 3/5 - The image has some material cues, but the pixelation reduces the effectiveness of the cues.
   - Noise/cluster quality: 2/5 - The image has a significant amount of noise and clustering, which detracts from the overall quality.

6. VERDICT: This image should not ship as-is. The pixelation and lack of detail make it less effective and detract from the overall quality. To improve it, the image should be upscaled with a focus on increasing detail and reducing pixelation. Additionally, refining the facial features and enhancing the shading and highlights would contribute to a more polished and engaging image.
