2. BIGGEST PROBLEM: The biggest visual problem is the lack of a clear, distinct head and face, which makes it difficult to identify the character as a knight.

3. TOP 3 CHANGES:

   - Refine the sword and shield to make them look more like traditional knight's weapons.

4. SILHOUETTE TEST: If all interior color was removed and only the outline remained, the shape would still read as a robotic knight. The silhouette is recognizable due to the distinct armor and weapon shapes.
